About Us

Since its founding in 2006, Yeongshin Korea has been importing and supplying food and ingredients from major suppliers worldwide to the Korean market.

Based on 20 years of international business experience and partnerships with leading global food companies, we have established a stable and efficient distribution channel from overseas to Korea. This foundation ensures reliable quality and adherence to the best practices in international trade for our clients.

We currently supply high-quality products to over 50 clients, including major food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners in Korea, providing more than just an intermediary service.

We have built strong partnerships with our suppliers and leveraged our years of accumulated experience and knowledge to develop a successful product portfolio, delivering greater value beyond mere product sales.
