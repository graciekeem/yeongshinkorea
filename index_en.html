Company Overview

Since its foundation in 2006, Youngshin Korea has specialized in importing food ingredients from global suppliers and delivering them to leading food manufacturers in Korea.

With 20 years of international business experience and trusted partnerships with world-class suppliers, we have built a stable and efficient channel that connects overseas producers with the Korean market. This ensures reliable quality and establishes exemplary practices in international trade.

Today, Youngshin Korea supplies high-quality products to more than 50 domestic clients, including food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners. We provide services that go beyond simple distribution, acting as a long-term business partner in the Korean market.

Through strong partnerships with our suppliers and years of accumulated expertise, we have developed a successful product portfolio and deliver value that extends far beyond sales, helping our partners grow and succeed in Korea.
