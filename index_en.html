Company Overview

Since its establishment in 2006, Yeongshin Korea Co., Ltd. has been importing and distributing food and ingredients from major global suppliers.

With over 20 years of international business experience and collaboration with world-leading food companies, we have built a stable and efficient distribution network in the Korean market, setting a model for cross-border trade.

In Korea, we supply high-quality products to over 50 clients, including food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners. Internationally, we build a successful product portfolio based on strong partnerships with our major suppliers.

Yeongshin Korea is not just a product distributor; through years of accumulated experience and expertise, we provide a higher level of value to both our customers and partners.
