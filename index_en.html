About Us

Since its establishment in 2006, Yeongshin Korea has been importing and distributing food and food ingredients from major global suppliers.

With over 20 years of international business experience and partnerships with leading global food companies, we have built a stable and efficient distribution network in the Korean market, setting a benchmark for international trade.

Domestically, we supply high-quality products to more than 50 clients, including food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners. Internationally, we have built a successful product portfolio based on strong partnerships with key suppliers.

Yeongshin Korea does not merely distribute products; with years of accumulated experience and expertise, we provide enhanced value to both our customers and partners.
