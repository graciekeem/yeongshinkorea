About Us

Since its establishment in 2006, Yeongshin Korea has been importing and supplying food and food ingredients to the Korean market from major suppliers worldwide.

Based on 20 years of international business experience and partnerships with leading global food companies, we have established a stable and efficient distribution channel from overseas to Korea. This foundation allows us to provide our clients with reliable quality and best practices in international trade.

We currently supply high-quality products to over 50 clients, including major Korean food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners, offering more than just an intermediary service.

We have built strong partnerships with our suppliers, and based on years of accumulated experience and knowledge, we have established a successful product portfolio, delivering greater value beyond simple product sales.
