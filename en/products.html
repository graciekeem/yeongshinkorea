Imported Products

We import and stably supply various fruit and vegetable concentrates—including apple, grape, and strawberry—from leading global manufacturers to domestic beverage and food companies.
Each product uses high-quality ingredients cultivated in the climate and soil of its origin, preserving the natural flavor and nutrients of the fruit through low-temperature concentration technology. We offer high-concentrate products and various concentration levels suitable for different uses, handling only products that have passed rigorous quality inspection.
It can be widely used in beverages, confectionery, baking, sauces, jams, and more, with customized supply available upon customer request.
