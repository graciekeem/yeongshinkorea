About Us

Since its establishment in 2006, Yeongshin Korea has been importing and supplying food and ingredients from major suppliers around the world to the Korean market.

Based on 20 years of international business experience and partnerships with leading global food companies, we have built a stable and efficient distribution channel from overseas to Korea. This enables us to provide our customers with reliable quality and best practices in international trade.

Currently, we supply high-quality products to over 50 customers, including leading domestic food processors, manufacturers, and brand owners, providing more than just brokerage services.

We have established strong partnerships with our suppliers. Based on years of accumulated experience and knowledge, we have curated a successful product portfolio and deliver greater value beyond simple product sales.
